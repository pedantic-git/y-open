Welcome to Yorkshire O.P.E.N!

We are a support community for people in or curious about ethically non-monogamous relationships.

We've been going since 2012 and welcome members and newcomers from across Yorkshire and the north of England.

Our main support & community meetup is online on Zoom on the evening of the first Thursday of every month. We also regularly meet up in person for members of our community to socialise.
