Zoom details

Before this pandemic, our meetups usually took place at the Victoria Hotel in Leeds city centre, and we hope to be back there soon.

Until then, we are meeting on Zoom.

Zoom is videoconferencing software you have probably heard of! You can download and install the software on PC, Mac, iPhone, iPad or Android.

The link to the call will be posted in the event page on Meetup well in advance of the call. Look for the section marked "Online event" - it's on the right hand side on a computer and near the top on a mobile - and click the link. You can find the Meetup event pages for our next few events below:

    If you have any trouble getting into our Zoom call, drop the organisers a message and we'll try to help you.
